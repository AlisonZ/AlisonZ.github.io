I am a linguist turned Software Engineer transitioning back to linguist. My initial work in linguistics research was focused on language documentation and revitalization. As a software engineer I have been working in the corporate sector honing my skills as an engineer. 

In 2025 I began the Master's Program in Computational Linguistics at University of Washington. I am looking to merge the skills I have garnered in my engineering career to be able to assist me in linguistic endeavors. My goals and focus is to enable communities in the preservation, discovery and revitalization of their languages. I am looking forward to using computational linguistics techniques and approaches to supplement this.

I am deeply interested in the ethics and morality of using large language models and "AI". As these tools are being adopted in a widespread fashion, I am interested in how we can enact policies that keep us and our data safe.
